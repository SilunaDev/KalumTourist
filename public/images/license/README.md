# License Images Folder

## Instructions
Place your 2 license images in this folder:

1. **license1.jpg** - Your first license (e.g., Tour Guide License)
2. **license2.jpg** - Your second license (e.g., Transport License)

### Image Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- **Recommended Size**: 1200x1600px (portrait) or 1600x1200px (landscape)
- **File Size**: Keep under 1MB for optimal performance
- **Quality**: High resolution for clarity when enlarged

### Current Structure:
```
public/
└── images/
    └── license/
        ├── license1.jpg (Add your first license here)
        └── license2.jpg (Add your second license here)
```

Once you add your images, they will automatically appear in the License section with:
- Professional certificate frame design
- Decorative colored corners
- Verified badge
- Click to enlarge functionality
- Hover zoom effect
